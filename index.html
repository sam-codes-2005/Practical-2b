Top 4 Places to Visit in Chandigarh
Saksham
Last updated September 25, 2024

1.

Rock Garden

Location: Sector 1, Chandigarh

Famous for its sculptures made of recycled materials, a great place to enjoy art and nature.

2.

Sukhna Lake

Location: Sector 1, Chandigarh

Perfect for a relaxing walk, boating, and enjoying the sunset.

3.

Rose Garden

Location: Sector 16, Chandigarh

One of the largest rose gardens in Asia, filled with vibrant colors and fresh air.

4.

Elante Mall

Location: Industrial Area, Phase 1, Chandigarh

A top destination for shopping, dining, and entertainment.
